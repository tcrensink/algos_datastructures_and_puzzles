Given an NxN matrix (nested list), print the NW to SE diagonal values on a new line, starting from the NE most corner down to the SW most corner.  For example:


    M = [
     [11 12 13 14],
     [21 22 23 24],
     [31 32 33 34],
     [41 42 43 44],
    ]

output:

    14
    13 24
    12 23 34
    11 22 33 44
    21 32 43
    31 42
    41
